Interesting Facts about the Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about the Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex & intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are reowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about the Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Adult pandas can eat 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Pandas like to lick copper and iron.

Interesting Facts about the Cicada

  • There are around 3,000 cicada species.
  • Cicadas have a lifespan of about 4-17 years.
  • Some cicada calls can be heard up to 1 mile away!

Interesting Facts about the Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour!
  • There are over 160 words for camel in Arabic alone.

Interesting Facts about the Teddy

  • Teddy Bears got their name from president Teddy Roosevelt (Theodore Roosevelt).
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  • The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
